In Conclusion.

Summarize the enduring legacy of the Soviet Union’s collapse: The collapse of the Soviet Union has had lasting effects on global politics, economics, and society. It marked the end of a bipolar world and the beginning of a new era of international relations.

Reflect on the lessons learned: The importance of understanding history to navigate current global politics is paramount. The collapse teaches us about the potential consequences of political and economic decisions, and the need for adaptability and resilience in the face of change.
